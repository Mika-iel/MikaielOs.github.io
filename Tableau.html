This project focuses on analysing sales and customer performance trends using Tableau. The objective was to compare year-to-year business performance across key metrics, including sales, quantity, profit, customers, and orders.

The project consists of two interactive dashboards:

  • Sales Dashboard – Focused on sales, profit, quantity, and category performance
  • Customer Dashboard – Focused on customer behaviour, customer distribution, and top-performing customers

The dashboards analyse company performance between 2021 and 2023 and allow users to interactively filter results by year, products, and locations.

Credits for the dataset and project inspiration go to Data with Baraa.

The data preparation process included:

  • Creating the data model within Tableau’s data source environment
  • Structuring the dataset using dimension tables (Customers, Locations, Products) and a fact table (Orders)
  • Creating relationships between tables
  • Checking and correcting column data types

The dashboard development process included:

  • Creating a year selection parameter for year-to-year comparisons
  • Developing calculated fields to compare selected year against the previous year
  • Building KPI calculations for total sales, quantity, profit, customers, sales per customer, and total orders
  • Ensuring KPIs dynamically update based on filters

The Sales Dashboard includes KPI cards, category performance charts, time-series trend analysis, weekly performance trends, and year-over-year comparisons with interactive filters and tooltips. The Customer Dashboard includes customer KPIs, distribution analysis, and a top 10 customers table to identify high-value customers.

The dashboards were enhanced through custom formatting, improved spacing and layout, removal of unnecessary gridlines, and consistent styling across all visuals. Titles, labels, and tooltips were refined to improve clarity and user experience.

Overall, the final dashboards provide an interactive experience that allows users to explore business performance trends dynamically.
